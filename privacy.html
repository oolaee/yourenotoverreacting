Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Overview

Should I Worry? ("the App") is developed by Dr. Oliver Morris. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App operates entirely on your device. All data you enter (child profiles, assessment history, medication timers) stays on your iPhone or iPad and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

No Accounts Required

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use it.

Subscriptions

Should I Worry? Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Subscription management and billing are handled by Apple. See Apple's terms for details.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or third-party services. No cookies, no fingerprinting, no device identifiers are collected.

Children's Privacy

While the App is designed to help parents assess their children's symptoms, it does not collect any data about children. Child profile names and ages are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted.

Data Storage

All app data (child profiles, assessment history, settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard data frameworks. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

Medical Disclaimer

The App provides general health guidance based on published medical literature and clinical protocols. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare provider with any questions about a medical condition. Call 911 for life-threatening emergencies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
