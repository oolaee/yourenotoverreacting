PRF vs PRP Economics

  • PRF commands a premium over PRP, consistently. Market data shows +$100–300 per session; a conservative, competitive premium is +$50–150.
  • Why the premium holds (sales narrative): no anticoagulant additive (all-natural), slower / sustained growth-factor release via the fibrin scaffold, "newer / advanced" positioning, and higher processing overhead (specialized horizontal centrifuge, tighter timing).
  • Protocol norm: 3–4 initial sessions, 4–6 weeks apart, then maintenance every 6–12 months. This makes packages the core revenue unit.
  • Package discounts are modest — typically 5–15% off single-session price for the initial series (e.g., Skin Pharm ~8%, Menz Orlando ~12.5%). Deep discounting is not required.
  • Financing: CareCredit and HFD (hair-specific) are the standard; prepay packages function as the de facto "commit & save" offer.

Suggested Pricing for a Palm Harbor PRF Launch

  • Single PRF session: ~$750–950. Sits at or just above local PRP, justified by the PRF premium narrative. Pricing under LivingYoung's $800 follow-up while offering PRF (which they do not) is a strong wedge.
  • Initial 3-session package: ~$2,100–2,400 (~10–15% off single). Matches LivingYoung's $2,100 anchor and St. Pete Wellness package math.
  • Maintenance: $500–700 every 6 months, ideally structured as a membership / subscription.
  • Differentiator: publish the price. The vast majority of competitors hide pricing behind a consult — transparent PRF pricing is genuinely rare in this market.

Methodology & Caveats

Thirty-six clinics were identified across the Tampa Bay market via structured web research in May 2026, filtered to hair-restoration (scalp) pricing only and excluding facial/skin PRF pricing. Most clinic websites returned HTTP 403 to automated retrieval, so several prices (Aria, Medspa of Tampa, LivingYoung, St. Pete Wellness) are drawn from search-engine snippets of those clinics' own pages rather than a live page read. Items marked "High" / "Medium" confidence are the most reliable; all prices should be confirmed by phone before finalizing. No local clinic publishes a PRF-specific hair price. The Pramah "~$600" figure is from a third-party aggregator and should be treated as soft. Figures are point samples, not verified rate cards, and reflect a snapshot in time.
